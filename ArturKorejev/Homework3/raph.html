Raphael (Raph) - The team bad boy, Raphael wears a dark red mask and wields a pair of sais. He is offten depicted with a Brooklyn accent. His personality can be alternately fierce and sarcastic, and often times deadpan humor. Still, he is intensely loyal to his brothers and sensei. He is named after the Italian painter and architect of the High Renaissance, Raphael.
