The book whitch impressed me in 2000th and slightly opened the door to the IT world is "IBM PC for User":   



What is this book about:

The book contains a detailed and accessible to beginners description of the capabilities of computers, their devices, programs, local and global networks, - techniques for proper operation in the DOS and Windows environment, ways to use the most popular and useful programs - Norton Commander, Norton Utilities, LEXICON, Aidstest, Dr. Web, ABinf, Norton Backup, Norton Antivirus, etc. For experienced users there are chapters on computer maintenance procedures, information security, disk recovery, virus protection, DOS and Windows configuration, etc. The book includes many illustrations and tables. The book will be useful for beginners and for experienced users of IBM PC-compatible computers.
