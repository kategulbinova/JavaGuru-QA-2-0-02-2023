Hamsters are small rodents in the Cricetinae subfamily. They are typically stout-bodied, with tails shorter than body length, and have small, furry ears, short, stocky legs, and wide feet. They have thick, silky fur, which can be long or short, colored black, grey, honey, white, brown, yellow, red, or a mix, depending on the species. Hamsters are commonly kept as pets.
